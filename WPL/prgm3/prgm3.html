About Perinthalmanna

Perinthalmanna is a beautiful town located in Malappuram district, Kerala, India. It is known as the Hospital City of Kerala because of its world-class healthcare facilities. The town is also famous for its rich cultural heritage and picturesque surroundings.Perinthalmanna is a rustic municipality that attracts people for its old-world charm. The place creates an ambience that takes visitors to an ancient and unique way of life.

Top Tourist Attractions

  • kodikuthimala: is a scenic hill station located in the Malappuram district of Kerala, India The peak has an elevation of 540 meters (1,770 ft) and is the highest point in the Amminikkadan hills. The name Kodikuthimala comes from the British, who hoisted a flag ("kodi kuthi" in Malayalam) on the hilltop during a survey. The hill station offers stunning views from a watchtower located at the peak. The surrounding area has been earmarked by the Tourism Department for development. The trail is also popular for hiking and birding. The area is considered a moderate hike, taking nearly four hours to complete the trail. It is accessible from Perinthalmanna, via Amminikkad.
    Read More

  • Keralamkundu Waterfalls: The waterfall cascades from approximately 1500 feet above sea level into a natural pool and is known for its rich biodiversity and picturesque surrounding forests. The monsoon and post-monsoon seasons offer the best flow and a vibrant, green landscape. Visitors can enjoy a dip in the natural pool, explore the surrounding forests, and engage in trekking.
    Read More

  • Paloor Kotta Falls: is a secluded, two-decker waterfall(The waterfall drops in two stages, creating a majestic cascade). known for its verdant surroundings and a trek through the woods. It is named after the area, which was once believed to be a refuge for the ruler Tipu Sultan. The best time to visit is during the monsoon season, when the waterfall is at its most magnificent.
    Read More

  • Kodikuthikallu:a scenic viewpoint and tourist attraction in Perinthalmanna. known for its breathtaking mountain views and mist-covered landscapes. It's a popular spot for adventure seekers, offering treks, such as the one from Kuranganchola, and providing a refreshing experience, especially during the early morning.

Major Medical Cares

Perinthalmanna offers comprehensive medical care includes government facilities and numerous specialty clinics for eye care, dental services, orthopedics, and alternative medicine like Ayurveda. You can also find specialized centers for heart conditions, fertility, physiotherapy, and speech and hearing, as well as advanced diagnostic and wellness services.



  • KIMS Alshifa Hospital:is a multi-disciplinary, 500-bed super-specialty hospital in Malappuram, Kerala. It has been a leading tertiary care provider in the region for over 35 years.Located over 9 acres with a built-up area of 3.5 lakh square feet, the hospital is well-equipped with modern facilities. Over 35 specialty and super-specialty departments, including cardiology, gastroenterology, endocrinology, and neurology. Multiple modern operating theaters and a variety of surgeries, including joint replacement, microvascular surgery, and spinal surgery.
    Read More
  • Moulana Hospital:is a 468-bed super specialty hospital located in Perinthalmanna, Kerala. It was established in 1989 by the Moulana Charitable Trust and is accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH). The hospital's nursing college is affiliated with the Kerala University of Health Sciences (KUHS).
    Read More
  • MES Medical College Hospital (MES MCH): is a multi-specialty hospital located in Perinthalmanna, Malappuram, Kerala. It was established in 2003 and is part of the MES Academy of Medical Sciences, a 60-acre campus that also includes dental and nursing colleges.: The hospital is equipped with a modern cath lab for cardiac surgery, a dialysis unit, and specialized units for laparoscopic surgery and other procedures.
    Read More
  • EMS Memorial Co-operative Hospital: is a multi-disciplinary super-specialty hospital named after former Chief Minister E. M. S. Namboodiripad. It is a significant hospital in the cooperative sector in Kerala. Affiliation with EMS College of Nursing and EMS College of Paramedical Sciences, providing various medical and paramedical courses.

Education in Perinthalmanna

The town is an educational hub in Malappuram. Popular institutions include:

  • Government & Established Colleges:
    •
    • Pookoya Thangal Memorial Government College: A government-affiliated institution offering degrees in Arts & Science.
    • MES Medical College: A prominent medical college in the area.
    • MEA Engineering College: Known for its B.Tech and other engineering programs.

  • Paramedical & Health Sciences:
    •
    • Al Shifa College of Paramedical Sciences: Offers paramedical courses and is associated with the Kerala University of Health Sciences.

  • Arts & Science:
    •

Transportation & Connectivity

Perinthalmanna is well connected by:

  • Road: NH966 connects the town to major cities.
  • Rail: Nearest station is Angadippuram Railway Station.
  • Air: Closest airport is Calicut International Airport.
